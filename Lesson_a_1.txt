Operation: Background activity without defined start or end.

Project: Endevour with defined object, start time, and end time. 

Projects: 
	- Have a unique purpose. 
	- Is temporary. 
	- Drives change and enables value creation. 
	- Is developed using progressive elaboration. 
	- Should have a primary customer and sponsor. 
	- Involves uncertainty. 

Project mamagers work with project sponsors, teams, and other people involved in the project to achieve project goals.
